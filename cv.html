Research Interests

  • Architectural Informatisation & Pre-Construction: Ethnographic study of design software (CAD, BIM, generative AI), embodied skill, and professional judgment in Korean architectural studios.
  • Digital Platform Labour & Creative Economies: Platform capitalism, creative precarity, and work routines of digital content creators (webtoon artists).
  • Archival Infrastructures & Institutional Practice: Database design, cataloguing protocols, museum loan networks, and the technopolitical systems shaping cultural memory across state institutions.
